## Summary

Remove the separate tab bar row from the TUI and merge tab indicators into the stats bar (topline). Change tab switching shortcuts from `1`/`2`/`3` to `F1`/`F2`/`F3` to avoid conflicts with form text input. The layout gains 2 extra rows for body content.
